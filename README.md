# UK Landlord Compliance Tracker

Free tool for UK landlords to track EICR, Gas Safety, EPC, and smoke alarm compliance deadlines.

## 🎯 The Opportunity

**November 2026 Deadline:** All UK rental properties must have electrical safety certificates (EICR) checked by this date.

**Market Size:** 2.6 million landlords in the UK

**Pain Point:** 
- Landlords hate paperwork and compliance tracking
- Missing deadlines means fines up to £30,000
- Existing solutions cost £20-50/month or are complex property management systems

## 🚀 Features

- **Property Dashboard:** Add and manage multiple rental properties
- **Compliance Tracking:** Track EICR, Gas Safety, EPC, smoke/CO alarms, Right to Rent
- **Deadline Alerts:** Visual warnings for expiring certificates (30/90 day warnings)
- **Stats Overview:** Quick view of compliant, warning, and overdue properties
- **Mobile-Friendly:** Works on phone/tablet for on-the-go checks
- **100% Local:** Data stored in browser, no accounts needed

## 📅 Regulatory Context

### EICR (Electrical Installation Condition Report)
- Required every 5 years
- Must be carried out by qualified electrician
- Fines up to £30,000 for non-compliance
- **November 2026 deadline** for all existing tenancies

### Gas Safety Certificate
- Annual check required
- Must be Gas Safe registered engineer
- Fines up to £6,000

### EPC (Energy Performance Certificate)
- Minimum E rating required
- Valid for 10 years
- Fines up to £5,000

### Smoke & CO Alarms
- Smoke alarms on each floor
- CO alarms in rooms with solid fuel
- Must be tested at tenancy start
